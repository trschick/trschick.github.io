You speak of justice. But what is it? Is it merely the interest of the stronger? Or is it something more?

Some say that justice is giving to each what is owed. But what is owed to a man? And who is to decide?

Is it just to harm your enemies? Or is it just to do good to all men, friend and foe alike?

Perhaps justice is a kind of harmony. A harmony of the soul, and a harmony of the city.

Let us reason together on this. For the love of wisdom is the love of justice.
